BECOME A LOCAL GUIDE

You know your neighbourhoods, right? Have you ever wondered why people want to visit them? Your local school, small but cosy restaurants, your friends' grocery shops - all these are what experienced travelers want to come and see. Show them your neighbourhoods and earn a little bit extra by being a guide!

If you would like to be a guide, please go at end of the page and fill the form. You will be asked a couple of questions to allow you to create your account. Once created, We will contact you as soon as possible. You need to determine your expertise, for example if you are a local history expert, an adventure guide, a surfing partner, just want to be a drinking buddy, and so on.

You will then wait for the travelers to contact you through LOCAL TRAVO. You need to ask the travelers to agree to the terms and conditions, such as cost, souvenirs, language exchange and so on. Then you just have to wait for the travelers to arrive in your neighbourhood!
